About

Hi. My name is Michael Li.

I’m an engineer, working primarily on software tools and automation infrastructure. I have dabbled in a variety of programming languages and tools. I’m trying to figure out how to do my part to make the world a better and happier place.

On the side, I enjoy a variety of activities, including sports (tennis, basketball), music recording, video production, community service, and gaming (Overwatch!).

This site’s purpose is to document what I’ve learned and share projects that I’ve worked on. I hope that the information here is useful and easy to understand, enabling others to make progress on whatever they are pursuing.

This blog uses the Jekyll platform, is hosted on Github Pages, and uses a customized Dinky theme.
