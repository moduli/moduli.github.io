Acoustic

Best Places to be a Mom

This song is a cover of Taking Back Sunday – Best Places to be a Mom. This was the second attempt at recording in separate tracks. I liked how the harmonies in the beginning of the chorus turned out, but the vocal part at the end of the song is a little cringe-inducing.

I Will Weigh 180 by Summer

This song is a cover of Taking Back Sunday – 180 By Summer. This was the first song that my brother and I tried to record in separate tracks. Drums were added via Fruity Loops (we may have gone overboard with some of the drum fills). There’s also a lot of clipping in the last section of the song.
